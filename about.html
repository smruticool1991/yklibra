ABOUT YK LIBRA

Yawan Kissan Library For Information & Broadcasting Resource Authority (YKLIBRA) is a not-for-profit organization and also non-political Organization. It was established on 26th April, 1992 in Dhenkanal district of Odisha. After working for few years, YKLIBRA expanded its operation to all over Odisha and other states. The focused working areas concentrated on Agriculture, Livestock, Fishery, Poultry, Climate Resilient Agriculture, Zero Budget Agriculture, Micro enterprise and Institutional Developments like SHGs, Farmers Clubs, Water User Groups (WUGs), FPOs, SHG Federation and Sustainable Development of rural poor in India.Our focused thematic area are Livelihood and Advocacy, Entrepreneurship Development, Child Right & Women Empowerment, Health & Education, Environment Conservation, Disaster Management , Governance & Institutional Building and Social Awareness

We’re here to support you every step of the way.
